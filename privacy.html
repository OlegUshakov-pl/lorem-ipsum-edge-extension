Privacy Policy for Simple Lorem Ipsum Generator

Last updated: 05 July 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Simple Lorem Ipsum Generator browser extension handles user data. We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that no personal information is collected, stored, or shared.

2. Data Collection

The extension does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data. Specifically, the extension does not collect:

  • names, email addresses, phone numbers;
  • browsing history;
  • IP addresses;
  • device identifiers;
  • any content typed or viewed by the user.

3. How the Extension Works

The extension generates placeholder text (Lorem Ipsum) locally in the browser. All operations occur entirely on the user's device. No data is sent to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension does not use:

  • analytics services,
  • advertising networks,
  • tracking scripts,
  • external APIs.

5. Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, no data is shared with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

The extension does not collect any personal information from children or adults.
